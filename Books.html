Terratron: Gods of Mars Vol - 1

Earth is dying, and humanity teeters on the brink of extinction. Faced with inevitable collapse, the brightest minds of a waning world embrace a daring solution: terraforming new worlds within the Milky Way Galaxy.

Self-replicating robots are dispatched across the stars, transforming barren planets into habitable oases. Now, brave teams of cosmic explorers, led by Captain Chris Vega and General Manander Crowe, venture to claim these new frontiers, beginning with Mars.

In the race to colonize the cosmos, humanity’s greatest strength becomes its deadliest weakness. The Milky Way Galactic Empire is vast, but cracks are beginning to show.
