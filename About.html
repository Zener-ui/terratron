WORLD BUILDING

The Science Behind the Story

What is Terraforming?

Terraforming is a process of changing the planet’s condition and making it habitable for plants and animals of planet Earth.

Space Colonization

Space colonization transforms planets into livable environments through human expansion beyond Earth.

Science Fiction & Reality

Sci-fi ideas are becoming reality thanks to advancements in space exploration and technology.

Multi-Planet Species

Humanity must evolve into a multi-planet species to survive long-term cosmic challenges.

THE AUTHOR

Nathaniel Bernadeau

Nathaniel Bernadeau brings a bold vision of humanity’s future to life with the Terratron saga. Drawing from real science, astrophysics, and a deep love of classic science fiction, he crafts worlds that feel both awe-inspiring and terrifyingly possible.

The Terratron series is his magnum opus — a multi-volume epic that explores not just the stars, but the very nature of what it means to be human when everything familiar is left behind.
